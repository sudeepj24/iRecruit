Product Name: iRecruit
Product Type: AI-Powered Recruitment Platform
1. Purpose of the Product
iRecruit is an AI-powered recruitment platform designed to reduce manual
hiring effort and accelerate the hiring process for modern HR teams. The
platform automates key stages of recruitment including candidate sourcing,
screening, outreach, evaluation, and shortlisting.
The goal is to help companies move from slow, resume-heavy hiring workflows
to fast, data-driven, and objective decision-making.
2. Problem Statement
Recruitment today is inefficient, repetitive, and biased toward volume rather
than quality.
Hiring teams face three major problems:
1. Too much manual effortRecruiters spend most of their time screening
resumes, sending follow-ups, and scheduling interviews instead of making real
hiring decisions.
2. Slow hiring cyclesIt takes weeks to move from job posting to final shortlist,
causing companies to lose good candidates.
3. Lack of objective evaluationShortlisting is often based on inconsistent human
judgment rather than structured data and clear scoring.
iRecruit solves these problems by automating repetitive work and introducing
AI-driven evaluation.
